The Three Platforms
🔗
Partner Network
ProLnk
The B2B backbone. Connects home service businesses into a cross-referral network where every completed job generates leads for partner companies.
  • Partner onboarding, tiering & commission management
  • AI-powered opportunity routing by category & proximity
  • Admin Command Center with full network visibility
  • Leaderboard, analytics & performance alerts
  • Stripe Connect automated commission payouts
  • Integrations: Jobber, ServiceTitan, CompanyCam
👥  Home Service Business Owners
📱
Field Operations
FieldOS
The technician app. Field crews log jobs, upload photos, and trigger AI analysis — automatically generating referral opportunities without any extra work.
  • Mobile-first job logging with 1-tap photo capture
  • Real-time earnings dashboard & tier progress
  • Route management & job scheduling
  • Inbound lead notifications & acceptance flow
  • Homeowner confirmation photos (trust builder)
  • Offline-capable PWA — works in the field
🔧  Field Technicians & Crew Members
🛡️
Consumer Platform
TrustyPro
The homeowner portal. Homeowners upload photos of their property, AI identifies what needs attention, and they're matched with verified local professionals.
  • AI home scan — upload photos, detect service needs
  • Searchable directory of verified, insured pros
  • Background-checked partner trust signals
  • Request a quote directly from any matched pro
  • Leads flow to ProLnk at full commission
  • No referring partner split — 100% margin to ProLnk
