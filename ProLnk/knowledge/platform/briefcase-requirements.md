# Briefcase Requirements by Property Type

## Residential Properties (Standard)
Minimum Briefcase requirements to receive residential leads:

| Document | Minimum Requirement |
|----------|---------------------|
| General Liability Insurance | $1,000,000 per occurrence |
| Workers Compensation | Required if any employees (exempt if sole owner-operator) |
| Contractor License | Required for licensed trades (HVAC, electrical, plumbing) |
| W-9 | Required before first payout |

## Commercial Properties
Additional requirements for commercial property work:

| Document | Minimum Requirement |
|----------|---------------------|
| General Liability Insurance | $2,000,000 per occurrence |
| Workers Compensation | Required |
| Commercial Auto Insurance | Required if company vehicles used |
| Bonding | $10,000+ depending on project size |
| OSHA Compliance | At least 1 OSHA-10 certified employee required |

## School Districts / Educational Facilities
Enhanced requirements for access to K-12 school properties:

| Document | Minimum Requirement |
|----------|---------------------|
| General Liability Insurance | $2,000,000 per occurrence |
| Workers Compensation | Required |
| All active Pro Passes on site must have: | Background check clear (within 2 years) |
| | Sex offender registry clear |
| | OSHA certification preferred |
| Company | Must have passed School Clearance review in Briefcase |

Individual Pro Passes needed for school work:
- Criminal background check (no felonies, certain misdemeanors may require review)
- Sex offender registry check
- Identity verification
- OSHA-10 or OSHA-30 preferred

## Healthcare Facilities (Hospitals, Medical Clinics)
| Document | Minimum Requirement |
|----------|---------------------|
| General Liability Insurance | $2,000,000 per occurrence |
| Workers Compensation | Required |
| Drug screening may be required | Per facility requirements |
| HIPAA awareness | May be required |

## Government Properties (Federal/State)
| Document | Minimum Requirement |
|----------|---------------------|
| General Liability Insurance | $2,000,000+ |
| Workers Compensation | Required |
| E-Verify compliance | Required for federal contracts |
| Government contractor registration | May be required |

## Briefcase Score Thresholds

| Score | Status | Access |
|-------|--------|--------|
| 0-29 | Draft | No leads |
| 30-59 | Restricted | Residential only, limited |
| 60-79 | Active | Full residential access |
| 80-89 | Active+ | Residential + commercial |
| 90-100 | Verified | All property types including school/healthcare |

## Document Verification Turnaround
- Standard documents (COI, W-9): 1-2 business days
- License verification: 2-3 business days  
- Background checks (via Checkr): 1-5 business days depending on complexity
- School/healthcare clearance: Up to 5 business days (requires enhanced review)

## Pro Pass Clearance Levels
1. **Residential** — Basic background check + license
2. **Commercial** — Enhanced background check + OSHA preferred
3. **School** — Criminal + sex offender + identity verification + OSHA
4. **Healthcare** — School requirements + drug screen may be required
5. **Government** — Most stringent — all of above + additional review
