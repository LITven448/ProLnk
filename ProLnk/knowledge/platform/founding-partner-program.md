# ProLnk Founding Partner Program

## What Is a Founding Partner?

A Founding Partner is one of the first 100 licensed, verified home service professionals to join ProLnk. This is a PERMANENT STATUS — not a tier, not a title. Once the 100 slots fill, the enrollment window closes forever.

Founding Partners are the people who believed in the platform early, helped build the network, and get rewarded for that for the life of their membership.

## The 9 Founding Partner Benefits

### 1. Locked Subscription Rate — $149/month forever
Gets the $249/month feature set (Company+/Elite plan) at the $149/month founder price. This rate is locked permanently and cannot be changed even as ProLnk raises prices. After 90 days free trial at launch.

### 2. Maximum Commission Keep Rate
Founding Partners keep 78% of every referral commission — the highest rate on the platform (normally Enterprise tier). Every job a partner in their network completes, the founding partner's originating commission keeps 78%.

### 3. Network Job Commissions — from ProLnk's platform fee
Every time a pro in the founding partner's network closes a job, the founding partner earns a % of ProLnk's platform fee:
- Level 1 (directly recruited pros): 7% of platform fee per job
- Level 2 (their recruits' recruits): 4% of platform fee per job
- Level 3: 2% of platform fee per job
- Level 4: 1% of platform fee per job
- Stops at Level 4 — this is not an MLM, it's a fixed-depth network program

**CRITICAL NOTE:** These cuts come from ProLnk's share of the fee. They do NOT reduce what the doing pro earned or what the homeowner paid.

**Example:** $5,000 HVAC job with 12% platform fee = $600 fee
- Pro who did job keeps 55% of $600 = $330
- ProLnk's gross share = $270
- FP (Level 1 above that pro) earns: $600 × 7% = $42
- FP at Level 2: $600 × 4% = $24
- Total to FP network: $66
- ProLnk net: $270 - $66 = $204

### 4. Network Subscription Commissions — monthly, recurring
Founding Partners earn from their network's monthly subscriptions:
- Level 1: 12% of monthly subscription (base only, not add-ons)
- Level 2: 6%
- Level 3: 3%
- Level 4: 1.5%

Applies to any subscription tier (not just $149). If their Level 1 recruit is on the $299/month plan, they earn 12% × $299 = $35.88/month.

### 5. Home Origination Rights — 1.5% of platform fee, forever
Every address a founding partner documents becomes permanently theirs. They earn 1.5% of every platform fee on every job at that address, forever, as long as:
- The house is documented in the ProLnk system
- The originating founding partner remains an active member

This applies even if ProLnk corporate later convinces the homeowner to join TrustyPro — the original documenting partner is still the originator.

**Example:** Partner documents 200 homes in DFW over 3 years. Each home gets $800 average in annual platform fees. At 1.5%, that's $200 homes × $800 fees × 1.5% = $2,400/year in pure origination income.

### 6. Priority Lead Routing
Highest PPS (Partner Priority Score) weight in the network. When multiple partners match a lead, founding partners get first offer.

### 7. TrustyPro Verified Badge
Top-tier verification badge — the highest credibility marker available on the platform. Displayed on their profile, visible to homeowners when they receive a deal card.

### 8. Founding Partner Events & Experiences
Exclusive gatherings, experiential rewards, and direct access to ProLnk's founding team. This is a community of 100 people who built something together — there are real relationship benefits.

### 9. Platform Development Input & Early Access
First to see new features. Founding partners vote on platform direction. Direct feedback loop to the product team.

## What's Required of Founding Partners

Founding Partners must:
1. Have an active contractor license (verified)
2. Pass a ProLnk background check
3. Carry general liability insurance (verified COI)
4. Complete full onboarding (profile, service area, Briefcase docs, Stripe Connect)
5. Add a minimum of 15 homes to the system
6. Recommend a minimum of 5 service professionals to join ProLnk
7. Pay $149/month subscription (after 90-day free trial at launch)
8. Remain an active participant — feedback, jobs, referrals

These aren't arbitrary — they're the minimum contribution needed to build a high-quality network that actually works. Founding partners who don't remain active will have their status reviewed.

## Network Commission Rules

- Network commissions come from ProLnk's share, not from the doing pro's earnings
- Maximum 4 levels deep — stops there intentionally
- This is NOT a multi-level marketing scheme — there are no recruitment fees, no buying product, no pyramid structure. It's a referral program with depth.
- Commissions run as long as the founding partner maintains an active subscription
- If a founding partner becomes inactive/cancels, their network commissions pause (not eliminated — they can reactivate)
- Network is built organically through referrals, not purchased

## Home Origination Rules

- First partner to document an address = permanent originator
- Corporate ProLnk cannot displace an existing originator
- If a homeowner finds ProLnk independently without a partner referral, and no partner has previously documented that address, ProLnk is the originator (no commission paid)
- Origination rights transfer to heirs if the founding partner account is properly transferred
- Origination rights are suspended (not eliminated) if the founding partner cancels their subscription
