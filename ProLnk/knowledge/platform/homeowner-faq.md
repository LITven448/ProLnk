# TrustyPro Homeowner FAQ

## General Questions

**What is TrustyPro?**
TrustyPro is a platform that helps homeowners document, protect, and maintain their homes. Upload photos of your home and our AI identifies maintenance needs, improvement opportunities, and connects you with verified local contractors — all for free.

**How much does TrustyPro cost?**
TrustyPro is 100% free for homeowners. We never charge homeowners for the platform, the AI scan, the Home Health Score, or connecting with contractors. We earn a commission from service professionals when jobs are completed — so our interests are aligned with getting you the best outcome.

**What is the Home Health Score?**
Your Home Health Score (0-100) represents the overall condition of your home's major systems. 80-100 = excellent, 60-79 = good with some maintenance needed, 40-59 = several items require attention, below 40 = urgent repairs needed. The score updates every time a new photo is analyzed.

**What is the Home Passport?**
The Home Passport is your home's permanent service history record. Every repair, upgrade, and service visit is documented. When you sell your home, buyers can see the complete maintenance history — typically making homes sell faster and for more money.

## AI Analysis Questions

**How does the AI photo analysis work?**
You upload photos of your home's systems and surfaces. Our AI analyzes each photo for 50+ issue types, evaluates component condition, estimates ages, and identifies both repair needs and improvement opportunities. The analysis is completed in minutes.

**What kind of photos should I take?**
Take clear, well-lit photos of: your roof, exterior walls, HVAC unit, electrical panel, water heater, plumbing under sinks, all rooms, attic (if accessible), and any concerns you have. Wide-angle shots of each space plus close-ups of specific issues work best.

**Can the AI miss things?**
Yes. The AI can only analyze what's visible in photos. It cannot detect issues inside walls, below floors, or underground. Think of it as a very thorough visual inspection — not a replacement for a licensed inspector for specific purposes like a home purchase.

**Is my data private?**
Your home photos and information are encrypted and never sold to third parties. You control your data. You can request deletion at any time from your privacy settings. ProLnk only uses your data to provide you with better service recommendations.

## Contractor Questions

**How are contractors vetted?**
Every ProLnk contractor goes through:
1. Background check (criminal, sex offender registry)
2. License verification (where applicable by trade)
3. Insurance verification (COI with required coverage amounts)
4. ID verification
5. Review from past clients

They carry a "Pro Pass" digital credential — you can scan their QR code at the door to see their verified status in real time.

**Do I have to hire a contractor?**
No. You can receive the AI scan and Home Health Score without hiring anyone. If opportunities are found, you'll see them in your dashboard and can choose whether to request a quote.

**How does pricing work?**
Contractors provide quotes based on your specific situation. You're under no obligation to accept any quote. TrustyPro's platform fee comes from the contractor's side — homeowners never pay a markup or platform fee.

**What if I'm not happy with a contractor?**
Contact support@trustypro.io. We take contractor quality seriously — our network's reputation depends on every job being done right. Partners with poor reviews are removed from the network.

## Home Assessment Questions

**What is a ProLnk Scout assessment?**
A certified ProLnk Scout visits your property and documents all 12 major systems in a single visit. They use photos, notes, and AI analysis to produce a comprehensive Home Intelligence Report with your Home Health Score, prioritized repair list, and cost estimates.

**How much does a Scout assessment cost?**
Scouts set their own assessment fee, typically $150-350 for a standard home. Commercial and large properties are priced higher. The fee is paid directly to the Scout — ProLnk earns its commission when any jobs from the assessment close.

**After the assessment, do I have to use ProLnk contractors?**
No. The Home Intelligence Report is yours. You can use it to get quotes from anyone. ProLnk makes it easy to connect with verified contractors through the Bid Board, but it's always your choice.
