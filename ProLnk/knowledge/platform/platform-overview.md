# ProLnk Platform Overview

## What Is ProLnk?

ProLnk is a home service partner referral network. Service professionals earn commissions by generating AI-detected leads for other professionals in the network — automatically, from photos they're already taking.

**The core loop:**
1. HVAC tech fixes an air conditioner
2. While there, they take before/after photos (or their CompanyCam/Jobber syncs automatically)
3. ProLnk AI analyzes the photos and detects other opportunities at that property (fence needs repair, gutters clogged, roof showing wear)
4. Those opportunities get routed to specialized partners in the same ZIP code
5. When any partner closes a job, the original HVAC tech earns a referral commission
6. Zero extra work required from the field tech

## The Three Products

**ProLnk** (prolnk.io) — The partner network for service professionals
- Partners log jobs and upload photos
- AI detects cross-sell opportunities
- Lead routing to qualified specialists
- Commission tracking and automatic payouts

**TrustyPro** (trustypro.io) — The homeowner platform
- Homeowners get a free Home Health Score
- AI photo analysis of their home's systems
- Connection to verified, background-checked contractors
- Home Passport (permanent service history record)

**ProLnk Media** (prolnkmedia.io) — Advertiser platform for businesses wanting to reach homeowners
- Real estate agents, mortgage brokers, insurance agents, home warranty companies
- Three tiers: Connect ($199/mo), Preferred ($349/mo), Exclusive ($799/mo)
- Impression/click analytics, territory exclusivity

## The Scout Role

ProLnk Scouts are certified property documentation specialists. They visit a property and systematically document all 12 home systems (roof through crawl space) using the Scout App with AI analysis.

Output: A Home Intelligence Report with Home Health Score, prioritized repair list, and cost estimates. Findings are posted to the Bid Board where qualified partners bid on the work.

Scouts earn: Assessment fee (paid by homeowner) + origination commission on every job that closes.

## How Commissions Work

Platform fee: 12% of job value (tiers by job size)
Referring partner keeps: 40-78% of platform fee (depends on subscription tier)
ProLnk keeps: 22-60% of platform fee

Example: $3,000 fence repair, Scout-tier referrer
- Platform fee: $3,000 × 12% = $360
- Referring partner earns: $360 × 40% = $144
- ProLnk nets: $360 × 60% = $216

Payouts: Nightly via Stripe Connect. $25 minimum.

## Why This Works

Home services contractors already take job photos. CompanyCam alone has millions of job photos taken daily. ProLnk simply connects those photos to AI analysis that turns each one into potential revenue. The marginal effort for a field tech is near zero.

The homeowner gets value too — their property is being documented with every service visit, building their Home Passport automatically.

## What ProLnk Does NOT Do

- ProLnk does not hold homeowner money or payments
- ProLnk does not employ service professionals (they are independent partners)
- ProLnk does not guarantee contractor quality (vetting is done, but no guarantee)
- ProLnk is not a licensed home inspection service (Scout assessments are "property documentation")
