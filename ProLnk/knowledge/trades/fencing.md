# Fencing — Trade Knowledge

## Common Fencing Jobs in DFW
- **Cedar wood privacy fence (per linear ft)**: $20-45
- **Full fence replacement (standard 150 LF)**: $3,000-6,500
- **Fence repair (per section)**: $200-600
- **Chain link fence**: $8-20/LF
- **Wrought iron fence**: $25-75/LF
- **Composite/vinyl fence**: $20-45/LF
- **Gate installation**: $400-1,500
- **Post replacement**: $200-400 per post

## DFW-Specific
- **Cedar wood** is the dominant material in DFW residential fencing (~70% of residential)
- **Standard height**: 6 feet for privacy, 4 feet for decorative
- **High wind events**: DFW storms frequently damage/destroy wood fences
- **Storm damage**: Wind causes panel blowouts, post snapping. Often insurance claim.
- **Average fence age at replacement**: 15-20 years for cedar in Texas climate

## Storm Damage Detection (High Insurance Claim Rate)
After high wind events:
- Panels blown out or leaning
- Posts snapped at base (common failure point)
- Fence leaning toward or away from yard
- Gates damaged or detached

**If detected after a known storm event → flag `isInsuranceClaim: true`**
Insurance coverage: Wind damage to fences is typically covered under homeowners policies (up to the policy limit, often ACV for fencing).

## Detection Signals in Photos
- **Leaning fence** (posts rotting at ground level — ROUTINE to IMMEDIATE)
- **Missing panels** (IMMEDIATE)
- **Rotted wood** (gray, soft, gaps in boards — ROUTINE)
- **Gaps at base** (boards warped/shrunk — routine)
- **Damaged gate** (hinges, latch, frame — routine)
- **Posts at severe angle** after storm (IMMEDIATE)
- **Chain link sagging** (routine)
- **Missing section entirely** (IMMEDIATE)

## Wood Fence Age Signals
- **New (0-3 years)**: Bright wood, tight fit, no gaps
- **Mid-life (4-10 years)**: Gray patina, minor gaps, some board movement
- **End-of-life (10+ years)**: Significant graying, rot at base, multiple loose boards, posts leaning

## No License Required
Fencing generally does not require a state license in Texas. Cities may require permits for fences over certain heights.
