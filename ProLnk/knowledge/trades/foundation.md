# Foundation — Trade Knowledge (Texas-Specific)

## Why Foundation Is Huge in DFW
Texas clay soil (expansive soils) expands when wet and contracts when dry.
This causes significant foundation movement in DFW — one of the most active foundation markets in the US.
Nearly 60% of Dallas-area homes experience some foundation movement over their lifetime.

## Common Foundation Jobs in DFW
- **Foundation inspection**: $200-500
- **Pier installation (per pier)**: $500-1,000 (steel piers), $300-600 (pressed concrete piers)
- **Foundation repair (average home)**: $5,000-15,000
- **Extensive foundation repair**: $15,000-50,000+
- **Drainage correction**: $2,000-10,000
- **French drain installation**: $1,500-6,000
- **Mudjacking / slab lifting**: $500-2,000

## Detection Signs (Critical Detections)

### URGENT — Foundation Distress Indicators
- **Diagonal cracks at corners of door/window frames** — most reliable sign
- **Doors or windows sticking** — frame has moved
- **Visible gaps between wall and ceiling/floor** — structure moving
- **Cracks wider than 1/4"** — significant movement
- **Sloping floors** — foundation settling unevenly
- **Separation of brick from mortar** — exterior movement

### WARNING — Monitor These
- **Hairline cracks in concrete floor** — normal if thin, monitor if growing
- **Vertical cracks in brick** — movement may be occurring
- **Cracks around fireplace** — common stress point

### NOT Foundation (don't flag)
- **Settlement cracks in drywall** — common in new construction, minor
- **Horizontal cracks in brick** — more likely thermal expansion than foundation

## Age and Soil Signals
- **Post-tensioned slabs** (cables in concrete) — common in DFW post-1980s; repair is specialized
- **Expansive clay soils** (dark, sticky soil visible in photos) — high movement risk
- **Trees near foundation** — roots draw moisture, can cause differential settlement

## The Watering Solution
Many DFW foundation issues are caused by inconsistent soil moisture.
Recommend homeowners maintain **consistent watering year-round** with soaker hoses at the foundation perimeter — prevents dry-season shrinkage.

## Key Contractors
- Foundation repair requires no state license in Texas (just general contractor)
- But good reputation and warranty (25-year piers) are key differentiators
- Major DFW companies: Perma-Pier, Foundation Systems of Michigan, Ram Jack, Ground Doctor

## isInsuranceClaim
Foundation damage is generally NOT covered by standard homeowners insurance (it's a maintenance/soil issue).
Exception: sudden damage from a covered peril (burst pipe causing washout).
Do NOT flag foundation issues as insurance claims unless there's a specific covered event.
