# HVAC — Trade Knowledge

## What HVAC covers
Heating, Ventilation, and Air Conditioning systems. In Texas, cooling is critical (extreme heat). Heating is needed in winter (ice storms can freeze pipes and stress heating systems).

## Common HVAC Jobs in DFW
- **AC tune-up/maintenance**: $89-150, done in spring before heat season
- **Refrigerant recharge**: $200-400
- **AC replacement**: $4,000-8,000 for a standard home (2-4 ton unit)
- **Furnace replacement**: $2,500-5,000
- **Full HVAC system (AC + furnace)**: $6,000-12,000
- **Air handler replacement**: $1,500-3,000
- **Duct cleaning**: $300-500
- **Thermostat installation (smart)**: $150-350

## Age Signals
- **HVAC nameplate**: Located on outdoor unit. First 4 digits of serial number often = year of manufacture (varies by brand)
  - Carrier: 1st digit = week, 2nd-3rd = year (e.g., "0914" = week 09, year 2014)
  - Trane: 1st digit = decade code, 2nd-3rd = year, 4th-5th = week
  - Lennox: 1st digit = year, 2nd digit = month
  - Goodman: digits 3-4 = year
- **Typical lifespan**: 15-20 years (shorter in Texas due to heavy use)
- **Replacement trigger**: Age 15+ years, frequent repairs, high energy bills

## Texas-Specific HVAC Notes
- Texas requires HVAC contractors to be licensed by TDLR
- EPA 608 certification required for refrigerant handling
- Heat pump systems increasingly popular (efficient for Texas climate)
- Attic HVAC units common — check for condensate drain issues (clogged = water damage)

## Opportunity Detection Signals in Photos
- Outdoor unit with visible rust, bent fins, or debris accumulation
- Nameplate showing unit age 15+ years
- Filter visible and dirty/clogged
- Refrigerant lines with ice buildup (sign of low refrigerant or airflow issue)
- Yellow/brown staining on ceiling near vents (past water leak from condensate)
- Outdoor unit without clear 2-foot clearance on all sides

## License Requirements
- HVAC Contractor License from TDLR (required)
- EPA Section 608 Certification (required for refrigerant)
- Electrical permit may be required for installation work
