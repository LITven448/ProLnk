# Roofing — Trade Knowledge

## What Roofing covers
All roof systems including asphalt shingles, metal, tile, flat/TPO commercial, gutters, skylights, flashing, and related storm damage repair.

## Common Roofing Jobs in DFW
- **Roof inspection**: $150-350 (often waived if they do the repair)
- **Minor shingle repair**: $300-800
- **Partial replacement (storm damage)**: $3,000-8,000
- **Full replacement — standard home 2,000 sq ft asphalt**: $8,000-15,000
- **Metal roof — full replacement**: $15,000-30,000
- **Flat/TPO commercial roof**: $4-8/sq ft
- **Gutter installation (per linear foot)**: $8-15 with guards, $5-8 without
- **Gutter cleaning**: $150-300 per visit
- **Flashing repair**: $300-700

## Age Signals
- **Asphalt shingle lifespan**: 20-30 years (3-tab), 25-50 years (architectural)
- **Metal roof lifespan**: 40-70 years
- **Tile lifespan**: 50+ years
- **Age estimate by granule loss**: Heavy granule loss = end of life
- **Age estimate by cupping/curling**: Shingles cupping = moisture issue, near end of life

## DFW Storm Damage Patterns
DFW has 50+ hail events per year. Key damage indicators:
- **Hail damage**: Circular dents/bruises on shingles, knocked-off granules, dents on metal flashing/gutters/vents
- **Wind damage**: Missing shingles, lifted shingles, damaged ridge cap
- **Tornado damage**: Major structural damage, entire sections missing

## Insurance Claim Opportunities
Hail and wind damage are often covered by homeowners insurance. Key signals that make a claim viable:
- Hail dents visible on soft metals (gutters, downspouts, AC condenser fins, mailbox)
- Multiple shingles with circular bruising
- Ridge cap damage
- Missing shingles after a storm

## Texas Roofing Note
- **No state license required** for roofing contractors in Texas
- But most cities (Dallas, Plano, Frisco) require permits for full replacements
- HOAs may have restrictions on roofing material/color

## Opportunity Detection Signals in Photos
- Missing shingles (any)
- Granule accumulation in gutters (end-of-life signal)
- Moss or algae growth on roof
- Sagging areas or visible structural issues
- Flashing gaps or separated flashing
- Gutters full of debris or pulling away from fascia
- Downspouts disconnected or pointing into foundation
- Age 20+ years + any damage = replacement opportunity
